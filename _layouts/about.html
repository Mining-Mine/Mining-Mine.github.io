<Our Mission>

The mining industry is evolving with technological advancements and large-scale data integration, yet it lags in adopting real-time monitoring and regulatory innovations, leading to inefficient data practices and over-reliance on historical frameworks.

In Ghana, a major gold producer, illegal mining ("galamsey") continues to cause environmental and socio-economic issues, despite reforms aimed at improving regulation. This highlights the need for more advanced monitoring systems.

Our research focuses on improving mining supervision using cloud-based data analysis and scalable machine learning. By integrating multimodal data like satellite imagery, we aim to build a real-time, adaptive monitoring framework to enhance regulation and environmental management.
